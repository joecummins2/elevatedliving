Our Services

At Elevated Living Group, we specialise in providing tailored residential care and support for children with emotional and behavioural difficulties. Our services are designed to create stability, promote healing, and foster growth in a nurturing environment.

What We Offer

24/7 Residential Care
A safe, secure, and structured home environment with round-the-clock support from our trained staff.
Emotional and Behavioural Support
Personalised care plans to help children manage emotions, develop coping strategies, and build resilience.
Education and Skill Development
Collaboration with local schools and tailored learning plans to ensure every child’s educational needs are met
Therapeutic Interventions
Access to therapy sessions, including counselling, art therapy, and other evidence-based practices.
Life Skills Training
Empowering children to develop essential life skills required for successful and fulfilling life beyond the home.
Recreational Activities
Opportunities for creative expression, sports, and hobbies to encourage a sense of joy and achievement.
We believe that every child deserves a chance to shine.
Get in touch to see how we can make a difference in a child’s life today.
