Accessibility Policy

Website Accessibility Statement

At Elevated Living Group, we are committed to making our website welcoming and usable for everyone, including individuals with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments. We design and maintain our website with the goal of aligning with recognized accessibility best practices and the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, aiming for at least AA compliance.

How We Support Accessibility

Design & Layout

We use cascading style sheets (CSS) to manage the visual layout of our site. This approach means:

  • People using screen readers can access the page's content without unnecessary layout details
  • Visitors can adjust how the website appears through their browser's settings or custom stylesheets, enabling them to tailor their experience to their needs â€” for example, by enlarging text or adjusting contrast

Navigation

We place our main navigation at the top of each page for easy access. Users of screen readers can choose to skip directly to the main content through a 'Skip to content' link. Additional related links are positioned thoughtfully to follow the primary information.

Content

We strive to create content that is straightforward and easy to read. This includes using clear headings to structure information, descriptive link text that makes sense out of context, and providing alternative text for images to support screen readers.

Known Limitations

While we work hard to make our content clear and understandable, some areas may require technical knowledge due to the topics discussed. We test our site across a range of modern web browsers, though we acknowledge that older browser versions may not display the site perfectly. If you prefer, you can still access content by disabling CSS styling.

Browser Compatibility

Elevated Living Group recognizes that web pages may look slightly different depending on the device, operating system, and browser used. Our focus is on maintaining functionality and readability, rather than making every page look identical everywhere.

Feedback & Contact

If you have any trouble accessing any part of our website or would like to suggest improvements, please reach out to us at info@elevatedlivinggroup.com. We appreciate your feedback as we continue to enhance our digital accessibility for all.
