## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our contact forms or interact with our residential children's care services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## Information We Collect

### Contact Form Data
When you complete our contact forms, we may collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Organisation/role (if applicable)
- Message content and enquiry details
- Date and time of submission
- IP address (automatically collected)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring website
- Pages visited on our site
- Time and date of visit

## How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

### Primary Purposes
- **Responding to enquiries:** To answer your questions and provide information about our services
- **Follow-up communications:** To maintain contact regarding your enquiry and provide relevant updates
- **Service delivery:** To assess suitability for our services and facilitate placements where appropriate

### Secondary Purposes
- **Internal record keeping:** To maintain accurate records of communications and enquiries
- **Service improvement:** To analyse enquiry patterns and improve our services
- **Compliance:** To meet our regulatory and legal obligations

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following legal bases:
- **Legitimate interests:** For responding to enquiries and maintaining business records
- **Consent:** Where you have explicitly agreed to receive marketing communications
- **Legal obligation:** Where required for regulatory compliance in children's care services
- **Contract:** Where processing is necessary for service delivery

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with:

### Internal Sharing
- Relevant staff members who need access to respond to your enquiry
- Management team for service planning and quality assurance
- Designated safeguarding leads where child protection concerns arise

### External Sharing (Limited Circumstances)
- Regulatory bodies (such as Ofsted) when legally required
- Legal advisors for professional advice
- IT service providers who support our systems (under strict confidentiality agreements)
- Emergency services where there are immediate safety concerns

We will never share your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:
- To respond to and follow up on your enquiries
- To maintain business records as required
- To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- To resolve any disputes or enforce our agreements

The specific retention period will depend on the nature of your enquiry and any ongoing relationship with our services. We regularly review our records and securely delete information that is no longer needed.

## Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have the following rights:
- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectification:** Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
- **Restriction:** Ask us to limit how we use your data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent:** Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details above.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:
- Secure servers with encryption
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Staff training on data protection
- Secure disposal of data when no longer needed

## Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies to improve functionality and user experience. We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

## Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

Given the nature of our services, we may receive enquiries about children's care. We take extra precautions when handling any information relating to children and will only process such data where we have appropriate legal basis and safeguards in place.

## International Transfers

We primarily process data within the UK. If we need to transfer data internationally, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by UK GDPR.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date.

By ticking the marketing consent box on our contact forms, you agree to receive updates about our services, news, and relevant information. You can withdraw this consent at any time by:
